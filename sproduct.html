"Did You Know That There's A Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd"

$30.00

Product Details

Did You Know That There's a Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd is the upcoming ninth studio album by American singer-songwriter Lana Del Rey. It is set to be released on March 10, 2023, by Interscope Records and Polydor Records. The album features production by Mike Hermosa, Jack Antonoff, Drew Erickson, Zach Dawes, and Benji, and includes collaborations with Jon Batiste, Bleachers, Father John Misty, Tommy Genesis, and SYML. It was preceded by the release of the lead single and title track, "Did You Know That There's a Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd", on December 7, 2022.
